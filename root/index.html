About

Paige MacGregor is a Front End Web Developer located in San Diego, California. She recieved training from the San Diego Continuing Education Department, where she was enrolled in a year long Front End Web Development program. There she learned about applications, technologies and principles related to web development and design such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, website deployment, responive web design, information architecture, and user expericence. In that time, she has published two made from scratch websites, one she completed alone and one in a group. In her spare time she enjoys playing with her cat and working on fiber arts.
